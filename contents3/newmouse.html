Announcement: Change to mouse functioning

Previously in VPython:

  • the left mouse button was used to zoom into and out of the scene
  • the right mouse button was used to rotate the camera

This required dragging with the left button up, which is unusual.

Starting with Python 2.2, this functioning of VPython has been changed to:

  • hold down both mouse buttons to zoom (or the middle button or "wheel")
  • the right mouse button is used to rotate the camera, as before

This permits dragging with the left button down, as in most applications.
